Good Morning My Dear Pondati - No Overlap Version

How to use:
1. Extract/unzip this folder.
2. Put your photo inside the same folder.
3. Rename your photo exactly: wife-photo.jpg
4. Open index.html in Chrome, Safari, or your phone browser.

- Final message and button are now inside one clean bottom box.
- Header text is smaller and more balanced.
- Envelope tap still works.
